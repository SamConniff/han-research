get me

A collective intelligence of father figures around the world. Thousands of answers, 505 men, 25 countries. Deeply imperfect — that's the point.

Every answer is one man's words, unedited. Men spoke; this remembers.

who's behind it

Sam Conniff. A dad in south London who kept noticing the questions men don't get asked — so he asked them. Built alongside the people already doing the work, not instead of them.

how it works

505 men answered ten questions, anonymously. Their words stay exactly as written — never edited, polished or paraphrased. Nothing appears here until a human has read it.
